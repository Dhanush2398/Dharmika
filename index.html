About Us

Dharmika Foundation is committed to serving humanity and preserving nature. Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities, support environmental conservation, and provide aid to those in need. Through various initiatives, we strive to create a positive impact in society.

  •  Education Support
  •  Community Development
  •  Environmental Protection
  •  Empowering the Disabled
  •  Humanitarian Aid
  •  Social Upliftment

Join us in making a difference! Your support helps us reach more lives and build a compassionate world. Together, we can bring hope, care, and change.

We are Dharmika Foundation, Transforming Lives.

At Dharmika Foundation, we are committed to creating a compassionate and sustainable world through education, humanitarian aid, and social initiatives.
